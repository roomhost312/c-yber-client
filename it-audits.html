IT Audits

Identifying problem areas in your technology environment.

IT audits provide insight into potential gaps in your technology processes and procedures. Our consultants use common security controls, frameworks and industry best practices to conduct IT audits that reveal details about your:

  • Information security posture
  • Risk management
  • Control design and operation
  • Compliance with security requirements

Unicybers consultants identify your risks and provide recommendations for addressing them. With this information, you'll have the confidence to make the right adjustments to your administrative, physical, and technical controls in order to better meet business goals, reduce risk, and save money.

Why Choose Unicybers for Your Audit

At every step of the audit, Unicybers focuses on improving your security program. Our consultants interview an array of employees throughout various departments (i.e., HR, IT, Compliance) to gain insights that go beyond technology and into the heart of the business.

Building on the interview results, Unicybers validates that the team's view of the controls reflects what is written in the policies and procedures. Then we assess the controls in operation to determine whether they are meeting the intended purpose described in the policies and procedures.

When to perform an IT Audit:

  • When You Complete a New Internal Project
  • Verifying the security of new projects enhances their long-term viability.
  • When You Add a New External-Facing Service
  • Introducing a new web app or hosting environment can introduce new vulnerabilities.
  • When You Need Compliance Support
  • Industry regulations or customer requirements may dictate that you do an annual audit.
  • When You're Implementing Vendor Security Management
  • Securing your organization means confirming the security of your third-party vendors.
